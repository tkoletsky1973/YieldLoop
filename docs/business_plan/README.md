## Purpose of This Document

This business plan describes the **organizational, operational, legal, and commercial framework** for the YieldLoop system.

It exists to explain:
- Why YieldLoop exists as a business
- How it operates sustainably without compromising its design principles
- How responsibilities, authority, and risk are allocated
- How the system is positioned relative to users, regulators, and partners

This document is **not** a marketing pitch and does not replace the YieldLoop whitepaper.  
It is intended to complement the technical specification by addressing execution, governance, and business realities.

Section 14 provides a consolidated financial model including budget, funding strategy, and operating revenue mechanics.

## Design Principles

The YieldLoop business is governed by the same principles as the system itself:

- Explicit authorization over implied consent
- Deterministic execution over discretionary behavior
- Outcome recording over performance projection
- Legal and ethical restraint over growth incentives
- Long-term operability over short-term scale

The business model is intentionally constrained to preserve system integrity, user safety, and regulatory defensibility.
